Jiangping Zhu (朱江平) 🏔️

Associate Professor and PhD supervisor(博士生导师), College of Computer Science, Sichuan University

Associate Dean (on aid mission), School of Information Science and Technology, Tibet University

I received my dual Ph.D. degrees from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC) and University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2014. My research focuses on medical image analysis, multi-modal learning, computer vision, 3D imaging, and HDR imaging. Currently, I am serving an aid mission to Tibet University as Associate Dean(西藏大学信息科学与技术学院副院长（挂职）), while keeping my affiliation with Sichuan University.

Office: Wangjiang Campus West District Science and Technology Innovation Center 314, Sichuan University

Email: zjp16@scu.edu.cn
